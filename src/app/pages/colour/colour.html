Colour Colour is an important element of our brand identity. It forms an essential part of our visual communication.
Primary colours Our primary colour palette is made up of dark shades of the corporate red and a range of shades of grey going from black to white.
This core palette should always play a leading role in any Santander communication. Our secondary colours Colour can bring communications to life and – when chosen carefully – make something good, into something great. Sometimes we need more than our primary colours to make a design work. For example when creating a graph or chart, we might need more colours to make the information clearer. Digital application should be limited to graphic elements. For best accessibility, don't apply this palette to screen text. Choose from the additional colours below. Additional colours For greater accessibility across digital items, the following colours have been developed to complement our primary and secondary range. These should only be used when there's a requirement to add colour to screen text. You’ll need approval from the Brand Team before using the secondary colour palette. Get in touch with the team at BrandDesignApproval@santander.co.uk if you have any questions.
